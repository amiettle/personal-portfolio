Web Development

OOTify Web Application

Summary

OOTify is a clinically rooted mental health B2B hub that matches members to a host of clinical and sub-clinical interventions. The mobile web application allows for users to take initiative in their mental health journey by recommending interventions according to their phenotype.

Details

Role

UI Design, Front-end development

Team

Amie L., Kenny T., Peter S., Abhi M., Samuel G.

Scope

10 weeks (October - December 2022)

Tools

Adobe XD, Angular, Firebase, Git, Github

The Problem

Students on college campuses have diverse mental health needs. Counseling and psychological services are in high demand as rates of depression and other psychological disorders, especially among freshmen students, have been increasing over the past several decades. The ability of colleges to meet the demand for clinical services is limited and insufficient to the needs of the students.

Our Solution

Create a mobile web application through which users can access an online mental health support community that includes synchronous and asynchronous communication with licensed mental health therapists, as well as non-clinical support resources. Use psychological assessments in conjunction with algorithms to match each client with mental health interventions -- including a therapist if appropriate, as well as other subclinical resources -- increasing the odds of a good fit for the client and their needs.
